Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your habit data is stored locally on your device using localStorage. We don't collect or store any of your personal information on our servers.

Can I export my habit data?

Currently, we don't have an export feature, but we're working on adding this functionality in a future update.

How do I delete a habit?

To delete a habit, go to the Tracker page, find the habit you want to remove, and click the "Delete" button. You'll be asked to confirm before the habit is permanently deleted.

Will you add user accounts in the future?

We're considering adding optional user accounts in the future to enable cross-device synchronization, but we're committed to keeping a privacy-focused, local-first approach.
